Storage feasibility

A longer piece of work: a full techno-economic model, grounded in the same battery backtest as the trading strategies.

Substation to storage - techno-economic, NPV / IRR, 3 scenarios + sensitivities

Is it worth turning decommissioned Czech substations (transformovny / rozvodny) into grid batteries instead of demolishing them? The whole edge is the existing grid connection - it skips the multi-year connection queue and holds up even if balancing revenue goes to zero. The answer is a conditional yes: only for the larger distribution and transmission sites, and realistically built by the grid owner or a connection-holding partner - which is exactly the pattern now playing out at Chvaletice, Kladno and Vítkovice.

+€8.1m reuse NPV edge (base case) · +€6.0m even if balancing revenue → 0 · 20 MW / 40 MWh representative site

Day-ahead arbitrage is taken from my own LP battery backtest on realised CZ prices; balancing revenue, capex and the connection saving are parameterised from 2025–26 Czech market data, with explicit scenarios and sensitivities. The full model code is included in the appendix.
